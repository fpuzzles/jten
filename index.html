What We Offer

Complete Lawn Care,
Delivered with Pride

From weekly maintenance to specialized treatments — every service is performed by Joseph himself, with professional-grade equipment and deep local knowledge of Southwest Florida's unique conditions.

Lawn Mowing & Trimming

Clean, consistent cuts at the right height for St. Augustine grass in Florida's heat. Precise trimming along every fence, tree, obstacle, and border — no missed spots, no scalping.

  • Proper St. Augustine blade-height settings
  • Sharp, well-maintained blades every visit
  • Available one-time, weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly

Edging & Blowout

Crisp, defined lines along every driveway, sidewalk, and curb. After every cut, all clippings are blown clean off hard surfaces — your property left spotless, every single visit.

  • Precise edging on all hardscapes
  • Full blowout of driveways, walkways & patios
  • Included with every service visit

Weeding & Prevention

Hands-on weeding and targeted prevention to keep your lawn and beds free of Florida's stubborn tropical weeds — protecting your St. Augustine turf visit after visit.

  • Manual weeding of lawn and visible beds
  • Preventative treatment to stop regrowth
  • Safe for St. Augustine turf

Clipping Management

Your choice every visit — mulch clippings back into the lawn to return nutrients to the soil, or bag and haul them away for a clean, manicured finish. We do whichever you prefer.

  • Mulching returns nutrients to sandy FL soil
  • Bag & haul available for a tidy finish
  • Your preference, your call — every time
